Jobs

In an effort to assist Java talent and employers connect, COJUG is offering a free moderated jobs mailing list. COJUG has struggled since it inception with having members seeking employment and recruiters requesting names. The mailing list is a compromise that keeps the monthly meetings focused on relevant Java and related technology topics while still assisting members find employment.

Job Seekers

Start receiving job and contract postings anonymously via email. Subscribe to the jobs mailing list by filling out the form below.

Enter your email address:

If the form didn't send you a confirmation e-mail you can subscribe to the job mailing-list by sending an email to cojugjobs-request@freelists.org with 'subscribe' in the Subject Field OR by visiting your list page at http://www.freelists.org/list/cojugjobs

Recruiters

Send job and contract postings to cojugjobs@freelists.org with a job description in the body and an appropriate subject. Interested talent can respond via email reply or provided contact information. It is recommend posting include the job title or description in the subject.
